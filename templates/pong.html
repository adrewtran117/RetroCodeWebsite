How to Play Pong:

Materials Needed:

  • Printable Game Playing Field Sheet
  • Printable Game Pieces
  • Ball
  • "Paddle Piece"
  • Paper and Pencil

How to Play!

Thrower - Person who chooses which block the ball lands on
Receiver - Person who uses the game pieces to move the paddle to the spot.

  1. The Paddle starts in the middle block on the receiver's side.
  2. The thrower tells the other which block the ball will land on. It is their job to use the coding block pieces to move the paddle so that it will move to the slot in which the ball goes.
  3. If the code is wrong and the paddle does not end up where the ball is, then the other thrower gains a point.
  4. Both players take turns being the receiver and thrower.
  5. Set the amount of points needed to win and have fun!
Note: You can use a pencil to fill in or change numbers on the coding blocks.
